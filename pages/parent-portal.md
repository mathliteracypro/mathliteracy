# 👪 MathLiteracy Pro: Parent & Guardian Portal Guide

Welcome, Parent or Guardian! MathLiteracy Pro is designed not just for students and teachers, but to empower you with easy, transparent tools to support your child's mathematical development.

---

## 💡 Why Math Literacy Matters

In South African schools, **Mathematical Literacy (Math Lit)** is a vital, practical subject that equips students with real-world decision-making skills:
* Understanding tax brackets, inflation, and salary slips.
* Budgeting for households and businesses.
* Reading plans, designing maps, and calculating measurements.

Our platform breaks down these essential concepts into bite-sized, interactive lectures and exercises that match the South African CAPS curriculum guidelines.

---

## 📊 How to Monitor Your Child's Progress

You don't need a background in mathematics to check if your child is studying consistently! Follow these simple steps:

1. **Check Their Daily Streak**: Sit down with your child and open their **Dashboard**. An active **Streak Counter** (represented by a golden flame symbol) shows you how many consecutive days they have studied.
2. **Review Study Duration Graphs**: On their **Profile** tab, look at the interactive study graphs. This visual calendar logs exactly how many minutes they spent watching videos or solving questions each day of the week.
3. **Verify Completed Tasks**: Check their **Tasks** section. If they have assignments marked as "pending review" or "graded," they have submitted their homework. If there are red "overdue" indicators, encourage them to complete outstanding work!

## 🏆 Verifying Academic Certificates

When your child completes an entire CAPS topic (e.g. Financial Mathematics), the system rewards them with a **MathLiteracy Pro Certificate of Completion**:

* **Printed Evidence**: You can print this certificate to display their achievement.
* **Cryptographic Authenticity**: To prevent forgery and guarantee your child earned their credentials, every certificate carries a unique validation code (hash). 
* **Public Verification**: You can enter this code at the public link `https://mathlit.free.nf/verify/{code}` to instantly retrieve our official database record confirming your child's achievement, grades, and completion date.

## 🔒 Your Family's Data Privacy

We understand that child safety and data security are extremely important:
* **Strict Privacy Isolation**: Your child's profiles, emails, test grades, and uploads are private. Other students on the platform are strictly blocked from seeing their performance logs or contact details.
* **No Advertisements**: The portal is purely an educational site, free from targeted commercial ads.
* **No Shared Logins**: Secure credential authentication prevents password reuse and account takeover.
