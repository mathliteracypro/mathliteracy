# 🔧 MathLiteracy Pro: Troubleshooting & Self-Help Guide

Having technical difficulties? Most common glitches can be resolved in seconds. Use this self-help manual to resolve login problems, network issues, and browser permissions.

---

## 🔐 1. Login & Account Verification Issues

### Why is my account blocked with a "Verification Required" notice?
* **Reason**: To protect data security and ensure that parents/students use authentic emails, we require all users to verify their email addresses.
* **Solution**: Check your email inbox (and spam folder) for a message from MathLiteracy Pro. Click the link inside the email to authorize your account.
* **No email received?** Go to the login page and tap **"Resend Verification Link"**. Please wait 60 seconds before requesting another email.

### Forgot your password?
* On the login page, tap **"Forgot Password"**.
* Enter your registered email address, and you will receive a secure password-reset link.

---

## 📶 2. Offline Mode & Network Connection Errors

### Why does a "Connection Lost" banner appear?
* **Reason**: If you undergo load shedding or lose cell network reception, the platform detects that your internet has disconnected.
* **Solution**: Keep learning! MathLiteracy Pro is designed with offline resilience:
  1. You can continue reading cached syllabus articles and using active calculations widgets.
  2. The system queues any newly logged study times or notes locally on your device.
  3. Once your device reconnects to the network, the queued changes are automatically pushed to our databases without losing your progress.

---

## 📷 3. Browser Permission Blockers

Our interactive lesson player and settings portal may request browser permissions (such as utilizing the microphone or camera for video calls, or accessing geographic location to suggest province-specific CAPS school curriculums):

### How to unblock permissions in Google Chrome:
1. Tap the **lock icon** 🔒 on the left side of your browser's address bar (next to `https://mathlit.free.nf`).
2. Toggle the switches for **Camera**, **Microphone**, or **Location** to **"Allow"**.
3. Reload the page to activate your changes.

---

## 🔄 4. Video Player Buffering Issues

If your course lectures are constantly buffering or dropping frame rates:
- Tap the **gear icon** ⚙️ at the bottom-right corner of the video screen.
- Decrease the video resolution quality (e.g. from `1080p` to `360p` or `240p`). This drastically reduces mobile data consumption and guarantees smooth playback even on weak 3G signals.
