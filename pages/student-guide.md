# 🎓 MathLiteracy Pro: Student User Guide

Welcome, Student! This guide is designed to help you navigate your dashboard, excel in your lessons, submit practice tasks, and earn completion certificates.

---

## 🚀 Step 1: Navigating Your Dashboard

When you log in, your **Dashboard** is your personal command center. It gives you an instant, visual overview of your study progress:

1. **Daily Study Streak**: Displays how many consecutive days you have logged in to study. Keep your streak alive to stay on track for exam success!
2. **Dynamic Widgets**: Track your total watched study minutes, lessons completed, and earned CAPS completion badges.
3. **Recently Watched Lessons**: A quick-action card showing your last viewed video lesson. Click **"Resume"** to jump straight back to the player at the exact second you left off.
4. **Current Daily Tasks**: High-priority homework assignments and practice exam runs that need your attention.

---

## 📽️ Step 2: Learning in the Interactive Study Hub

Our interactive study environment combines video lectures, notes, and exercises in one unified space:

- **Autosave Playback**: Close your browser or turn off your computer during load-shedding without fear. The video player automatically remembers your exact playback timestamp.
- **Drop Bookmark (`Ctrl + B`)**: Found a complex finance formula or scaling chart you want to review later? Click **"Drop Bookmark"** or press `Ctrl + B` to pin that exact timestamp to your notes, along with your custom commentary.
- **Formulas & Study Material Tab**: Review structured markdown summaries directly below the video window, presenting South African CAPS formulae simply and elegantly.
- **Lesson Quizzes**: Test your knowledge instantly! Complete interactive multiple-choice tests directly within the study frame to confirm your understanding.

---

## 📝 Step 3: Completing Daily Tasks & Practice

To prepare for school-based assessments (SBAs) and final exams, you will complete scheduled curriculum tasks:

### Solving a Task
1. Navigate to the **Tasks** tab.
2. Select an active practice assignment.
3. Tap **"Start Focus Timer"** to trigger a 25-minute study run, hiding sidebars to help you avoid distraction.
4. Download related templates or spreadsheets, fill them out with your calculations, and save them.

### Submitting Your Work
MathLiteracy Pro supports flexible, simple file uploads:
* **Drag-and-Drop**: Drag your completed PDF document, Excel spreadsheet, or photos of your written notes directly into the upload area.
* **Manual File Browse**: Tap the upload window to select files from your computer or mobile device.
* Once uploaded, click **"Submit Assignment"** to send your work to your instructor for feedback.

---

## 🏆 Step 4: Tracking Milestones & Achievements

As you work through your topics, the platform gamifies your progress to keep you motivated:

- **Badges Feed**: Unlock visual, colorful badges like *Weekly Streak Master*, *Finance Champ*, or *Measurement Wizard* as you hit milestones.
- **Study Analytics Graphs**: The Profile page displays beautiful weekly study charts outlining your study patterns and minutes spent.
- **Verified Course Certificates**: Once you achieve 100% completion in a major CAPS topic (e.g. Mapwork), you earn an official **MathLiteracy Pro Completion Certificate**.
